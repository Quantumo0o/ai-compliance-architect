# Service Portfolio & SLA: Institutional AC Maintenance

## 🛠️ Comprehensive Maintenance Framework
ClimateTech Solutions India operates on a **Zero-Downtime** philosophy. Our maintenance framework is designed to exceed the "Comprehensive Maintenance" requirements of the RAK National Postal Academy.

### 1. Detailed Preventive Maintenance (PM) Checklist
| Task | Frequency | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Air Filter Cleaning** | Monthly | High-pressure air/water cleaning to ensure airflow. |
| **Drain Line Check** | Monthly | Clearing blockages to prevent water leakage in offices. |
| **Refrigerant Pressure** | Quarterly | Monitoring suction and discharge pressures (R-32/R-22). |
| **Electrical Audit** | Quarterly | Tightening terminals, checking MCBs, and measuring Amp draw. |
| **Chemical Wash** | Bi-Annually | Eco-friendly foam cleaning of condenser/evaporator coils. |
| **Blower Balancing** | Annually | Checking for vibration and lubricating fan motors. |

### 2. Service Level Agreements (SLA)
*   **Category A (Critical)**: Server rooms, VIP Suites, Conference Halls. **Response: 2 Hours.** **Fix: 12 Hours.**
*   **Category B (General)**: Classrooms, Staff Cabins, Dormitories. **Response: 4 Hours.** **Fix: 24 Hours.**
*   **Replacement Policy**: If a unit cannot be repaired within 48 hours, a **Standby AC** will be installed at no extra cost to the Academy.

### 3. Spare Parts & Consumables
Under the **Comprehensive AMC**, CTSI provides the following at no additional cost:
*   Compressors (Brand-matched).
*   Fan Motors and Blowers.
*   PCB Boards (Original or OEM-Equivalent).
*   Gas Charging (Full or Top-up).
*   Capacitors, Relays, and Thermostats.

## 📋 Technology-Led Maintenance
