# Company Profile: ClimateTech Solutions India

## 🏢 Executive Summary
ClimateTech Solutions India (CTSI) is an ISO 9001:2015 certified HVAC engineering and maintenance firm. Established in 2012, we have transitioned from a local repair shop to a multi-state service provider specializing in **Sustainable Cooling Operations**. We currently manage over 15,000 AC units across Northern India.

## 🎯 Mission & Vision
*   **Mission**: To provide uninterrupted comfort through data-driven preventive maintenance and rapid-response repair engineering.
*   **Vision**: To be the gold standard in institutional HVAC maintenance, leveraging IoT and green refrigerants for a cleaner environment.

## 🏗️ Technical Expertise & Infrastructure
Our firm provides comprehensive maintenance services for:
*   **Precision Cooling**: Specialized in Server Room cooling (AC-PAC units).
*   **Split & Multi-Split ACs**: High-efficiency units from 1.0 to 3.0 TR.
*   **Window AC Engineering**: Expert chassis restoration and noise-reduction servicing.
*   **VRF/VRV Systems**: Daikin and Mitsubishi certified service partners.
*   **Inverter Logic Experts**: Advanced PCB repair lab located in Ghaziabad for rapid turn-around.
