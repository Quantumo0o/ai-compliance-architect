# Safety, Health, and Environment (SHE) Policy

ClimateTech Solutions India is committed to the highest safety standards during the execution of AC maintenance at the RAK National Postal Academy.

## 👷 Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
All CTSI staff on-site must strictly adhere to the following PPE kit:
1.  **Safety Shoes**: Steel-toed, ISI-marked electrical resistant shoes.
2.  **Safety Helmets**: Mandatory during indoor/outdoor unit installation.
3.  **Full Body Harness**: Mandatory for any work above 2 meters (outdoor units).
4.  **Insulated Hand Tools**: Rated for 1000V to prevent arc flash injuries.

## ☣️ Refrigerant Handling & Environment
*   **Recovery Machines**: We use portable recovery units to prevent venting of gases (R-22, R-410a) into the atmosphere.
*   **Leak Detection**: Electronic halogen leak detectors used for precise troubleshooting.
*   **Cylinder Storage**: All refrigerant gas cylinders are stored in upright, ventilated lockers on-site (if space provided) or at our Ghaziabad depot.

## 🔥 Fire Safety & Hot Work
*   **Brazing Protocols**: "Hot Work Permits" are obtained before any copper pipe brazing.
*   **Fire Extinguishers**: Technicians carry portable CO2 extinguishers during brazing operations.

## ♻️ E-Waste Disposal
All replaced parts (Compressors, PCBs, fan blades) are collected and disposed of through **Authorized E-Waste Recyclers** as per the Govt. of India guidelines.
