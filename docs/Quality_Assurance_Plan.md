# Quality Assurance & Audit Plan (QAAP)

ClimateTech Solutions India (CTSI) follows a "Zero-Error" quality framework for all maintenance operations.

## 🔎 Multi-Tier Inspection Process
To ensure the RAK National Postal Academy receives the highest service quality, we implement a 3-layer audit:
1.  **Technician Self-Check**: Mandatory digital checklist completion via FieldApp after every service.
2.  **Supervisor Audit**: Weekly random inspection of 15% of serviced units.
3.  **Monthly Site Review**: Joint inspection with the Academy's Electrical/Estate wing to sign off on performance.

## 📊 Performance Tracking (KPIs)
We monitor the following KPIs for the Academy:
*   **Mean Time To Repair (MTTR)**: Target < 24 hours.
*   **Preventive Maintenance Adherence**: Target 100%.
*   **Repeat Complaint Rate**: Target < 2%.

## 💡 Customer Feedback Loop
*   **Service Rating**: On-site staff can rate every service via a QR-code link.
*   **Grievance Redressal**: Any rating below 3 stars triggers an automatic escalation to the CTSI Service Manager.

## 🔧 Technical Training Unit
*   **Upskilling**: All staff attend quarterly "Inverter Tech" workshops.
*   **Soft Skills**: Technicians are trained in "Institutional Etiquette" for working in active educational environments.
